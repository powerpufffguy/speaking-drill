🗞 Daily Speaking Drill Sunday, May 10, 2026
A man turns 100 — and he still loves nature
Real news · Beginner & Intermediate · 5 minutes · English
📰 The News (read aloud or listen)
David Attenborough is a famous British man who makes nature documentaries — films about animals and the natural world. This week, he celebrated his 100th birthday. He was born in 1926, before many modern inventions. People in the UK call him a national hero. He is still working today. His most famous show is called Planet Earth. Many people around the world love him.
📖 Key Vocabulary
documentary
a film or TV show about real life
"I watched a documentary about lions."
celebrate
to do something fun for a special day
"We celebrate birthdays with cake."
nature
animals, plants, and the outdoors
"I love spending time in nature."
hero
a person people respect and admire
"Teachers can be heroes too."
💬 Speaking Questions
1
How old is David Attenborough? What does he do?
⭐ Beginner — find the answer in the text
2
Do you like watching nature documentaries? Why or why not?
⭐ Beginner — personal answer, any words ok
3
Who is a person you admire? What do they do? How old are they?
⭐⭐ Intermediate — use full sentences
4
What do you think is the secret to a long and happy life?
⭐⭐ Intermediate — give 2 or 3 ideas
